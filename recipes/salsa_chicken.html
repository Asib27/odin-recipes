Pineapple Salsa Chicken

Description

It is a good recipe. Try it for once. Good luck cooking it.

Ingredients:

Chicken
  • 4 boneless(6 ounce) chicket breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon jamaican jerk oil

Salsa

  • 1 cup diced pineapple
  • 1/4 th cup dice red bell paper
  • 1 small jelapeno paper

Cooking procedure

  1. preheat an outdoor grill
  2. pour chicken
  3. pour salsa
